Garlic Herb Steak Foil Packs

    Ingredients
  • Steak
  • Red Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Red and Green Bell Peppers
  • Red Onion
  • Butter
  • Parlsley
  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Garlic Cloves(Whole)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
      • Directions
    1. Chop up Red Potatoes, Carrots, and Peppers
    2. Cut Steak in bite-sized pieces
    3. Toss Veggies with oil, salt and pepper then set aside
    4. Mix softened butter with herbs
    5. Place ingredients inside foil packs
    6. Grill for 15 mins
